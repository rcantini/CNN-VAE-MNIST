Let's play with MNIST

...

This application is based on a Convolutional Variational autoencoder trained on the MNIST dataset of handwritten digits.

It can be used in two ways:
- you can select the digit you want to be generated by the Variational autoencoder;
- you can pick-up a random point of the latent space and look at what digit appears.
